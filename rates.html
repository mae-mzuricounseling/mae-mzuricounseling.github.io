The practice accepts some insurance plans, including Aetna, Centivo, Cigna, Horizon BCBS, United Healthcare, Optum, Oxford, and Oscar Healthcare.

Rates

The standard rate for therapy sessions is $150. Cost for EMDR sessions may vary. Sliding scale options available for individuals seeking mental health care with financial difficulties. Please call to confirm.

Cancellation Policy

Full cancellation fee for appointments cancelled under 24 hours of scheduled session, 75% rates apply for cancellations under 48 hours.
